Offset Printing

Offset printing is a commonly used technique where the inked image is transferred from a printing plate to a rubber blanket and then to the printing surface.

At A.V. Print N’ Pack we use advanced Heidelberg printing machinery to deliver high-quality printing for various commercial and corporate requirements.

  • Brochures & Annual Reports
  • Product Catalogues
  • Posters & Flyers
  • Business Cards & Stationery
  • Corporate Documents
